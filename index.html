The Factory

Built like Warhol.
Powered by AI.

In the 1960s, Andy Warhol created the Factory in New York. A space where art, film, music, and culture were produced constantly, visibly, prolifically. The Factory was the brand. Not what it sold — what it made.

BLAST is that. In 2026. With AI at the core. One human with a clear vision — and a factory floor of specialized AI agents that can build anything, at any scale, faster than any traditional team.

Warhol had humans pretending to be machines. We have machines amplifying a human. That inversion defines this decade.

"Don't think about making art, just get it done. Let everyone else decide if it's good or bad, whether they love it or hate it. While they are deciding, make even more art."
